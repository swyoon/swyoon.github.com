I am a Ph.D. student at Robotics Laboratory (advisor: Prof. Frank Chongwoo Park) in Seoul National University. My adviosr is Prof. Frank Chongwoo Park, and I also work closely with Prof. Yung-Kyun Noh (Hanyang University). I am deeply interested in discovering principles of learning and using them to build better learning algorithms. My recent research aims to address the following questions:

  • Can we make a machine learning algorithm better by taking a probabilistic approach?
  • What are the limitations of the probabilistic approach in machine learning?
  • What are the uncertainties in machine learning and how can we measure them?
  • Can we make a better decision using the knowledge on the uncertainty?
